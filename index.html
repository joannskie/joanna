Joannalyn Oania's Career Aspirations

I have two siblings: Marjorie Oania, my younger sister in her final year of high school, and Joshua Oania, my youngest brother. Overcoming obstacles, whether academic, personal, or social, has instilled in me determination and adaptability in my educational and professional pursuits. My unique path, with its specific milestones and turning points, provides me with a perspective unlike any other.
